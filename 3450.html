(KOALA) Long Distance App + Product

Current long distance relationship products may not address the deeper aspects of communication needed in long-distance relationships, such as meaningful conversations and emotional support. These solutions contribute significantly to the realm of long-distance relationships. However, their limitations often stem from their focus, target audience, or lack of customization to address the wide spectrum of challenges and needs that long-distance couples face. Long-distance relationships require an all around approach that combines physical connection, emotional understanding, creative interaction, and communication customization for various types of relationships. Koala will provide these.
