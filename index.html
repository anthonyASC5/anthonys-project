Improving communication between college students in long-distance relationships by allowing for stronger passive conveyance of emotions and providing a creative bonding outlet

Design Problem

College students in long-distance relationships struggle to communicate adequately with their partners due to their busy schedules. Text, as their main mode of communication, is ineffective at conveying intangibles like emotions and tone of voice, leading to misunderstandings that make navigating long-distance relationships even more difficult.

User Research

LDR Couples primarily communicate and connect at night Most LDR couples engage in some virtual activity to maintain the ‘spark’ in their relationship Misunderstandings and miscommunications often arise in LDRs due to text being the primary mode of communication and interaction. It's difficult to convey emotional nuances through text alone, especially if couples don’t speak frequently throughout the day. Some partners enjoy the independence and space that come with an LDR, while others may struggle with feelings of clinginess. Expressing these differences can pose challenges for couples who haven’t communicated them.

JOBS365

Jobs365 is a project I worked on at All Star Code during my Summer intensive. Jobs365 is a resource for teens under 18 in need of employment - a common issue that I had faced. Jobs365 gives access to all types of jobs nearby the user, from companies and organizations to odd jobs and tutoring, so they always have an opportunity to work simply be a source of a job when needed. I want to continue learning and working to refine this project, including connecting with my community on posting job openings. Revenue and business will come from local companies who want their jobs posted and advertised on the website. Jobs365 website was made with Javascript, HTML + CSS. Click to visit Jobs365!
