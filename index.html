JOBS365

Jobs365 is a project I worked on at All Star Code during my Summer intensive. Jobs365 is a resource for teens under 18 in need of employment - a common issue that I had faced. Jobs365 gives access to all types of jobs nearby the user, from companies and organizations to odd jobs and tutoring, so they always have an opportunity to work simply be a source of a job when needed. I want to continue learning and working to refine this project, including connecting with my community on posting job openings. Revenue and business will come from local companies who want their jobs posted and advertised on the website. Jobs365 website was made with Javascript, HTML + CSS. Click to visit Jobs365! Jobs365
