(2023-2024) Nominated For Human Spirit Award

I am thrilled to share that I have recently been honored with the prestigious Human Spirit Award for my brand, ShotbyALL. This recognition represents a significant milestone in my journey as an entrepreneur and photographer, highlighting my dedication to making a positive impact through my work. I was nominated for this award through ECornell (Entrepreneurship at Cornell University.) I am deeply honored to have been nominated for this remarkable $5,000 opportunity to nurture and expand my brand.

*My photography style is deeply rooted in portraits and people-centric photography, characterized by the innovative use of fisheye lenses and wide angles to offer dynamic and unique perspectives.

*My work extends beyond the traditional boundaries of photography, seeking to engage viewers in a visual dialogue through every picture. I aspire to provide a platform for minority and underrepresented individuals, offering them the opportunity to see themselves in a positive light and fostering a sense of confidence that extends beyond the frame.

This is not only about photography, this is about my entrepreneurship skills of building a trademark and giving back to my community through creativity.
